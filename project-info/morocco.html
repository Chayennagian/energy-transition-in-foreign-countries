Grow of solar panels in the Netherlands

In 2004, only a few households had solar panels due to the installation costs of 1500 euros.

In 2016, solar power broke through in the rental sector of houses. This is mainly due to social pressure and the ambitions from the Energy Agreement.

The price of solar panels drops quickly due to better efficiency of solar panels and mass production in China. In 2012, a panel with a higher power yield cost 70 percent less than in 2008. At the end of 2012, there were suddenly 175,000 houses with solar panels.

From 2020 onward, you can purchase solar panels with a cheap loan, which you repay partly from the amount you save on electricity. By the end of 2020, 1 million homes had solar panels.

Currently, more than 36 million solar panels are installed in the Netherlands, on roofs or on the ground.
